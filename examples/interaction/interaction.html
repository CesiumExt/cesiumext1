This example shows how to use different interactions derived from CesiumExt.interaction.Interaction abstract class. abstract class:

  • Each Change in the Grid Panel will be reflected in Cesium.DataSource and Cesium.DataSourceCollection instances.
  • And each change on Cesium.DataSource and Cesium.DataSourceCollection will also be reflected in the Grid Panel.

Have a look at interaction.js to see how this is done.
